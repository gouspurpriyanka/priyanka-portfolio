Artifact 1

AI & Machine Learning Timeline

Research

A visual research artifact that traces major AI milestones, highlights critical breakthroughs, and strengthens my understanding of the field’s evolution.

Overview: This timeline explores the evolution of Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning, from the earliest concepts of intelligent machines to the modern era of deep learning and generative AI. Creating it helped me understand how decades of research and innovation shaped the technologies we use today.

Why I Created It: As I transition into AI-focused software engineering, I wanted to build a stronger understanding of where today's AI technologies originated. Rather than memorizing recent tools, I wanted to learn the milestones, breakthroughs, and challenges that influenced the field over time.

What I Learned: Researching this timeline showed me that AI has progressed through cycles of rapid innovation, setbacks, and renewed breakthroughs. It also reinforced how improvements in computing power, data availability, and algorithms have worked together to accelerate modern AI development.

How I Built It: I gathered information from course materials, academic resources, and trusted AI references before organizing the major events into a chronological timeline. My goal was to present the information in a way that is easy to understand while still highlighting the significance of each milestone.

Skills Demonstrated: Research • Technical Communication • Information Organization • Critical Thinking • AI Fundamentals

Professional Relevance: Understanding the history of AI gives me better context when evaluating modern technologies such as large language models, machine learning frameworks, and intelligent software systems. This foundation helps me make more informed technical decisions as I continue growing in the AI field.

Artifact 2

EatWise AI Help Proxy

Deployment

A live Next.js deployment that brands Chatbase help content into a product-style EatWise AI support experience with user-friendly design and seamless routing.

Overview: EatWise AI Help Proxy is a deployed Next.js application that provides a branded entry point for the EatWise AI help experience. It connects users to a Chatbase-hosted help page through a Vercel deployment.

Why I Created It: I created this artifact as part of my AI Lab work to show how an AI support resource can be packaged into a professional, user-facing web experience. Instead of only linking directly to an external tool, I designed a simple branded page that makes the help center feel connected to the EatWise AI product.

What I Learned: This project helped me understand how AI tools can be integrated into a web application workflow. I practiced configuring environment variables, using Next.js rewrites, deploying to Vercel, and troubleshooting production issues such as missing environment values and server errors.

How I Built It: I built a minimal Next.js app, configured a /help route to connect with the Chatbase help page, added a polished EatWise AI landing page, and deployed the finished project to Vercel for public access.

Skills Demonstrated: AI Tool Integration • Web Deployment • Next.js • Vercel Hosting • Debugging • User Experience Design

Professional Relevance: This artifact demonstrates my ability to move an AI lab activity beyond experimentation and into a usable product experience. It shows that I can connect AI services, web development, deployment, and user-centered design.
